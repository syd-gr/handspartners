河村治義
Haruyoshi Kawamura

代表取締役社長
Representative Director and President

「H&Sパートナーズ 」われわれはこの社名に、ホテル(H)とスタッフ(S)を大事にし、 互いにタッグを組んで、お客様に対して質の良いサービス(S)を提供し、 満足(S)していただきたいという思いを込めています。 われわれの仕事は、 関係するすべてのスタッフを信頼し、良い関係を維持しつつ、 ホテルを利用するお客様に対して質の高い清掃サービスを通して安心で快適な環境を提供することを目的とします。 H&S パートナーズ、われわれは変化する社会、地球環境においてますます重要となる相互の信頼関係を大事にし、 仕事に真摯に向き合ってまいります。

Our company name "H&S Partners" reflects our desire to value hotels (H) and staff (S), teaming up to provide quality service (S) to guests and ensure their satisfaction (S). Our work is aimed at providing a safe and comfortable environment for hotel guests through high-quality cleaning services, while trusting and maintaining good relationships with all staff involved. At H&S Partners, we value mutual trust, which is becoming increasingly important in a changing society and global environment, and we approach our work with sincerity.

We established H&S Partners with the goal of becoming a cleaning company that provides environment friendly, high-quality hotel room cleaning and satisfies our customers. Our motto is to provide our customers with what they need as quickly as possible, even if it's just a second faster, while thinking about their happiness.
